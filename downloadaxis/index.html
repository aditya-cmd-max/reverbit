Frequently Asked Questions

Is Reverbit Axis free to use?

Yes, Reverbit Axis is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required to access its core features. We believe in making powerful tools accessible to everyone.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All your work is processed locally whenever possible, meaning only you can access it. We use end-to-end encryption for cloud-synced data and never store your encryption keys on our servers.

Can I use Reverbit Axis on multiple devices?

Currently, Reverbit Axis is available only for Android devices. We're working on iOS and web versions that will allow you to use your account on multiple devices simultaneously with seamless sync.

How do I update the app?

When a new version is available, you'll receive a notification within the app. You can download updates directly from our website or through the app itself if you enabled automatic updates in your settings.

What makes Reverbit Axis different?

Reverbit Axis integrates all Reverbit products into one unified experience, offers offline functionality, prioritizes privacy with local processing, and provides Clover AI integration that you won't find in any other productivity app.
