Frequently Asked Questions

Is Exonova Axis free to use?

Yes, Exonova Axis is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required to access its core features.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All your messages and calls are protected with end-to-end encryption, meaning only you and the recipient can access them. We also never store your encryption keys on our servers.

Can I use Exonova Axis on multiple devices?

Currently, Exonova Axis is available only for Android devices. We're working on multi-device support that will allow you to use your account on multiple devices simultaneously.

How do I update the app?

When a new version is available, you'll receive a notification within the app. You can download updates directly from our website or through the app itself if you enabled automatic updates.
