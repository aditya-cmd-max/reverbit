Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Exonova products and services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Getting Started

How do I create an Exonova account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of our website, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You'll be ready to explore Exonova in under 2 minutes.

What are the system requirements?

Exonova works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on both Windows and macOS. For mobile access, we recommend using our iOS or Android apps for the best experience.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all new users with access to all premium features. No credit card required to start your trial.

Billing & Plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also accept cryptocurrency payments.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Plan changes take effect immediately with pro-rated billing adjustments.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain access until then.

Security & Privacy

How is my data protected?

We use enterprise-grade 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 Type II compliant data centers with regular security audits.

Do you comply with GDPR?

Yes, Exonova is fully GDPR compliant. We provide data processing agreements (DPAs) and have appointed a Data Protection Officer to ensure compliance with all relevant regulations.

Where is my data stored?

Customer data is stored in geographically redundant AWS data centers located in the United States and European Union, depending on your selected region during signup.

Features & Usage

How do I integrate with other tools?

Exonova offers native integrations with over 50 popular tools through our API and pre-built connectors. Visit our Integrations page for setup guides and documentation.

Can I use Exonova on mobile?

Yes! We have fully-featured iOS and Android apps available on their respective app stores. Our mobile apps offer offline capabilities and push notifications.

How often are new features released?

We deploy updates weekly with minor improvements and bug fixes. Major feature releases happen quarterly, based on customer feedback and our product roadmap.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to answer your questions and help you get the most out of Exonova.
